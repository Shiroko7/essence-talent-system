# Air Essence Abilities

## Initiate Tier

### Mistbound Step
```yaml
id: air_initiate_mistbound_step
tier: initiate
isActive: false
isPassive: true
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

The Misty Step spell does not consume a spell slot if you begin or end your movement in a heavily obscured area.

---

### Misty Veil
```yaml
id: air_initiate_misty_veil
tier: initiate
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can create a shroud of mist around you that obscures your appearance, granting advantage on Stealth checks to hide and disadvantage on Insight checks to discern your true intentions. This effect lasts for 1 hour.

---

### Thunderous Roar
```yaml
id: air_initiate_thunderous_roar
tier: initiate
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can let out a powerful roar that creates a shockwave, forcing all creatures within 10 feet to make a Constitution saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away from you and become deafened for 1 minute.

---

### Whispers of the Gale (Laura)
```yaml
id: air_initiate_whispers_of_the_gale
tier: initiate
isActive: false
isPassive: true
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

When you miss with a ranged attack roll (including spell attacks), you can use your reaction and expend a spell slot of 1st level or higher to reroll the attack. You must use the new roll, and the spell slot is expended regardless of the outcome.

---

## Adept Tier

### Echoing Footsteps
```yaml
id: air_adept_echoing_footsteps
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can move with such speed and silence that your footsteps echo faintly. You gain advantage on Stealth checks, and creatures within 10 feet of you have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks to hear you. This effect lasts for 10 minutes.

---

### Thunderstep
```yaml
id: air_adept_thunderstep
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can use an action to make a sudden, loud noise, creating a brief but intense thunderclap that can be heard up to 300 feet away. Creatures within 30 feet of you must make a Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of your next turn.

---

### Calm Breeze
```yaml
id: air_adept_calm_breeze
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can use a bonus action to create a soothing breeze that relaxes and calms creatures within a 30-foot radius, providing them with advantage on saving throws against being frightened or charmed. This effect lasts for 10 minutes.

---

### Misty Escape
```yaml
id: air_adept_misty_escape
tier: adept
isActive: false
isPassive: true
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

When you take damage, you can use your reaction to turn into a cloud of mist, causing the attack to miss. You must spend a spell slot of 1st level or higher to use this ability.

---

### Whispering Winds
```yaml
id: air_adept_whispering_winds
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can use your action to send a message carried by the wind to any creature within 1 mile of you. The message is heard as a whisper by the intended recipient. This ability allows for secret communications or covert information exchange.

---

### Wind Barrier
```yaml
id: air_adept_wind_barrier
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

As a reaction to being targeted by an attack or harmful spell, you can summon a barrier of wind around you. You gain a +2 bonus to AC and saving throws until the start of your next turn. Additionally, ranged weapon attacks against you have disadvantage.

---

### Cyclone Step
```yaml
id: air_adept_cyclone_step
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

When you use the Dash action, you can create a swirling vortex of wind around you. Creatures within 5 feet of your path must make a Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. The wind dissipates at the end of your movement.

---

### Wind Sprint
```yaml
id: air_adept_wind_sprint
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can double your movement speed for a number of rounds equal to your total of wind essences.

---

### Sound Analysis
```yaml
id: air_adept_sound_analysis
tier: adept
isActive: false
isPassive: true
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can accurately determine the source and direction of any sound within 300 feet of you. This ability can be used to track creatures or detect hidden enemies. It provides you advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks related to sound.

---

### Gale Force Strike
```yaml
id: air_adept_gale_force_strike
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

When you hit a creature of your size or smaller, or any creature one size category larger than you, with a weapon attack, you can create a gust of wind that forces the creature to make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, the creature is pushed 10 feet away from you.

---
