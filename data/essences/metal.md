# Metal Essence Abilities

## Initiate Tier

### Metalcrafting
```yaml
id: metal_initiate_metalcrafting
tier: initiate
isActive: false
isPassive: true
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

Gain proficiency with smith's tools if you don't already have it.

---

### Magnetic Attraction
```yaml
id: metal_initiate_magnetic_attraction
tier: initiate
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can use a bonus action to pull or push a metal object weighing up to 10 pounds up to 10 feet towards or away from you. This can be used to manipulate weapons, tools, or similar objects in combat or to solve puzzles.

---

### Metal Sense
```yaml
id: metal_initiate_metal_sense
tier: initiate
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can detect the presence of metal within 30 feet of you. You sense the type and general location of metal objects, including hidden compartments or concealed weapons.

---

### Keen Edge
```yaml
id: metal_initiate_keen_edge
tier: initiate
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

As a bonus action, you can temporarily sharpen a weapon, granting it a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls for the next hour.

---

### Magnetic Focus
```yaml
id: metal_initiate_magnetic_focus
tier: initiate
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

As a bonus action, you can magnetically attract a metal weapon you can see within 30 feet of you to your hand. This weapon does not provoke opportunity attacks while being drawn.

---

### Metallic Echo
```yaml
id: metal_initiate_metallic_echo
tier: initiate
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

When you strike a metallic object with a melee weapon, you can create a ringing sound that echoes in a 10-foot radius. Creatures in the area must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be deafened until the end of their next turn.

---

## Adept Tier

### Enhanced Armor
```yaml
id: metal_adept_enhanced_armor
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can spend 10 minutes to reinforce any armor. For the next hour, you gain resistance to one type of damage (chosen when you use this ability) while wearing armor.

---

### Magnetic Manipulation
```yaml
id: metal_adept_magnetic_manipulation
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You can use an action to exert magnetic force on a metal object or creature within 60 feet. You can move the object or creature up to 10 feet in any direction, or cause the object to be attracted or repelled by a metal surface.

---

### Gemsight
```yaml
id: metal_adept_gemsight
tier: adept
isActive: false
isPassive: true
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

You gain proficiency in Perception checks to locate hidden gemstones or precious minerals. When you find such items, you can appraise their value with a successful Intelligence (Investigation) check.

---

### Magnetic Shield
```yaml
id: metal_adept_magnetic_shield
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

As a reaction when you are hit by an attack, you can create a magnetic field that reduces the damage by 1d10 + your Constitution modifier. If the attacker is wielding a metal weapon, they must make a Strength saving throw or be disarmed.

---

### Steel Tornado
```yaml
id: metal_adept_steel_tornado
tier: adept
isActive: true
isPassive: false
isSpell: false
isCantrip: false
```

As an action, you can create a whirlwind of metallic shards in a 30-foot radius around you. Creatures within the cylinder must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 7d6 slashing damage, or half as much damage on a successful save.

---
